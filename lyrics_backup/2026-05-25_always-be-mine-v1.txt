[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the heat when you pull me close
The kind of fire that nobody knows
A midnight spark in the candle glow
I just want to take it nice and slow

[Verse 1]
We are tracing shadows on the bedroom wall
Caught in a feeling where we lose it all
A heavy gaze that is locking onto mine
We are pouring out another glass of wine

[Pre-Chorus]
A silent promise hanging in the room
The fading shadows like a sweet perfume
I lean in closer and I pull you near
There is nothing left for us to hide or fear

[Chorus]
You are the only one who holds my soul
You always spin me out of my control
I want your passion and I want your time
I swear to heaven you will always be mine

[Post-Chorus]
Always be mine
(Always be mine)

[Verse 2]

Watching the hours softly slip away

[Bridge]

We have completely vanished in the night
The only sound is beating from our chests
